RATES AND SERVICES

HULL CLEANING

Based on 2-month cleaning schedule, 18 month haul out schedule. Boats with poor paint condition and excessive fouling are subject to higher rates.


SAILBOATS

Less Than 30' - $3.50 / ft

Greater Than 30' - $3.75 / ft


POWERBOATS

Less Than 30' - $4.50 / ft

Greater Than 30' - $4.50 / ft


Our cleaning service uses the least abrasive scrub pad possible to avoid removing more paint than needed. This extends your paint life and improves your sailing and boat performance. This service includes cleaning all metal running gear, clearing thru hulls and communicating with you about what the condition of your hull looks like.
